  • Case Study - Patient Tracking System Hardware

Client Name: Regional Cancer Centre, Thiruvananthapuram

One of the major Hospitals in Trivandrum for Cancer treatment ran by the Govt. of Kerala. The Hospital handles about Thousands of patients per day.

Challenge

Regional Cancer Center of Thiruvananthapuram required reliable Tracking of Medical history of their patients for cancer treatment with Barcoding as the primary scope.

Solution Offered

  • Successfully Provided Barcode based solution.
  • Provided Unique ID Card for each Patient.

Business Impact

  • Better Tracking of Patient visits and their medical records.
  • Clear interface for analyzing the track records.
