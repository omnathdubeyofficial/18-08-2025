Company Overview

Since 1992, Markss Infotech Ltd has been a pioneer in Automatic Identification & Data Capture (AIDC), empowering businesses across India with barcode, RFID, IIoT, and digital transformation solutions. Based in Mumbai, with regional offices spanning New Delhi, Jaipur, Lucknow, Ahmedabad, Kolkata, Raipur, Nashik, Pune, Vishakhapatnam, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and Chennai, we deliver real time visibility and intelligent insight into asset life cycles and workflows Markss. Trusted by over 3,000 customers and partnering with leading global vendors like Zebra Technologies, Cognex, Advantech, SOTI, Omni ID, Diebold Nixdorf, NiceLabel, and more, our solutions power industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, retail, logistics, pharmaceuticals, and FMCG.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

1992–1995

Began as a barcode and automation consultancy.

1995 onward

Transitioned into a full-fledged AIDC solution provider, launching flagship products and forming global tech partnerships.

2000s–2010s

Expanded into multiple industries including manufacturing, pharma, healthcare, logistics, FMCG, and government. Introduced track and trace, WMS, MES, and automation systems.

2019

Received the Zebra APAC Award for excellence in AIDC across Asia-Pacific.

2021

Honored as Zebra Solution Partner of the Year for our innovation and customer-first delivery.

2022

Awarded Zebra Partner Solution of the Year – APAC, recognizing industry-leading, scalable deployments.

2024

Zebra Technologies Partner of the Year – ISC Region for excellence in reliable AIDC solutions.

2025

Zebra Partner of the Year. Strengthened leadership in end-to-end enterprise solutions.

Today

Serving 3,000+ clients, delivering 300+ projects across 10+ major cities — enabling seamless interoperability of physical assets & digital intelligence at scale.
